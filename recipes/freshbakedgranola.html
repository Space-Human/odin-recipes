Fresh Baked Granola

An adaptable recipe for addictively tasty vegan baked granola

Combine oats, nuts, fruit, peanut butter, and applesauce for an energy-packed snack

This granola can be eaten with yogurt, broken into clusters and eaten as breakfast cereal with your favorite plant-based milk, or simply munched on as is!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups oats
  • 1 cup trail mix
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1/4 cup applesauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Melt peanut butter in a pan with maple syrup.
  3. Mix in applesauce, oats, and trail mix.
  4. Spread in an oiled baking pan and bake until brown, about 30 minutes.
  5. Cut into bars or crush into clusters.
  6. Enjoy your delicious creation.

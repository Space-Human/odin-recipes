Frito Boats

A childhood favorite of mine, this quick and low-cost meal will hit the spot every time

This recipe combines salty, savory, spicy, and creamy flavors for a delicious and timeless result that will leave you wanting more.

An excellent "school night" meal as this can be thrown together in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag corn chips
  • 1 can zesty pinto/chili beans
  • 1/4 cup shredded plant-based cheese
  • sliced jalapenos to top
  • chopped green onions to top

Instructions

  1. Empty the can of beans into a pot and heat it up on the stove.
  2. Fill a bowl about halfway with corn chips.
  3. When the beans are hot, scoop them on top of the corn chips.
  4. Add shredded cheese on top of the hot beans where it will get nice and gooey.
  5. Top with jalapenos and green onions and dig in!

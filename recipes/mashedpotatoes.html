Mashed Potatoes

Creamy mashed potatoes using plant-based ingredients!

Easy to make, deliciously satisfying, and cruelty-free!

Ingredients

  • 6-8 yukon gold potatoes
  • 1/4 cup plant-based butter (melted/softened)
  • 1/4 cup oat milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp rosemary
  • 1/4 cup chives (optional topping)
  • water to cover

Instructions

  1. Slice potatoes into 1-2 inch pieces. Put sliced potatoes in a large pot and cover with water by about 1-inch.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat and cook for 30 minutes or until tender.
  3. While potatoes are cooking, chop your chives and melt your plant-based butter.
  4. Once potatoes are tender, drain the water using a strainer and place the potatoes back in the hot pot.
  5. Add melted butter, oat milk, and seasonings to the potatoes.
  6. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher and stir until well combined.
  7. Top with chives and serve!
